About Islamic Marriage

Marriage is a tradition of the Prophet (peace and blessings be upon him). Because there are around 1.8 billion Muslims, the traditions at the actual ceremony could be different with each family, culture, etc., but the fundamentals of getting married are the same.
The Nikah ceremony is a Muslim marriage ceremony in which the bride and the groom say "I do" and the marriage contract is signed.
Nikah ceremonies usually take place in a masjid or a hall with an imam officiating it. Usually, couples will invite their family and friends.
